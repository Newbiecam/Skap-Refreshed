About
Who's behind refreshed.pw

refreshed.pw is an independent dev project with one focus: taking software people already love and making it feel the way it always should have.

Thoughtful redesigns

Every project is rebuilt with care — not just reskinned. UI, flow, and feel are reconsidered from the ground up.

Performance first

Lean and fast. No unnecessary dependencies — just the code that needs to be there and nothing more.

Always iterating

Projects here are never truly "done" — they ship early and keep getting better with every update.
