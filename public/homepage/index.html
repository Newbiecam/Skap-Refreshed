About
Who's behind refreshed.pw

refreshed.pw is an independent dev project with one focus: taking software people already love and making it feel the way it always should have. No bloat, no compromise — just cleaner builds, sharper design, and a genuine respect for the player.

Thoughtful redesigns

Every project is rebuilt with care — not just reskinned. UI, flow, and feel are all reconsidered from the ground up to actually improve the experience.

Performance first

Refreshed projects are lean and fast. No unnecessary dependencies, no heavy overhead — just the code that needs to be there and nothing more.

Always iterating

Refreshed builds, constant improvements. Projects here are never truly "done" — they ship early and keep getting better with every update.
